What is Ladakh?

Ladakh is a beautiful region in northern India, known for its breathtaking landscapes, ancient monasteries, and rich culture. Located between the Great Himalayas and the Karakoram mountain ranges, Ladakh is a place of untouched beauty and peace.

Top Destinations in Ladakh

Destination Description Best Time to Visit
Leh The capital of Ladakh, known for its beautiful monasteries and vibrant culture. May to September
Pangong Lake A stunning saltwater lake that changes colors and is located at the border with China. May to October
Shanti Stupa A peaceful Buddhist shrine that offers panoramic views of Leh and the surrounding mountains. Year-round

Things to Do in Ladakh

In Ladakh, you can enjoy trekking, mountain biking, river rafting, and exploring Buddhist monasteries. The serene environment and majestic landscapes provide a perfect backdrop for adventure and relaxation.
